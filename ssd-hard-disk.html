Various Types of SSD Available in BD

SSDs come in various types and sizes. SSD can be classified based on their interface and form factor. Based on form factors, SSDs come in two main types, 2.5-inch and M.2 SSD. In terms of interface, SSDs mainly use three types: SATA, NVMe (PCIe), and mSATA. Below are the common types of SSD available in Bangladesh:

2.5-Inch SATA SSD

The most popular type of SSD is the 2.5-inch SATA, which is compatible with a range of desktops and laptops. 2.5-inch SSDs use the SATA interface and connectors, making them compatible with any Desktop PC or laptop already using a hard disk drive. SATA SSD is ideal choice for a storage upgrade as it offers faster read and write speeds compared to traditional HDDs. With transfer speeds of around 500-600 MB/s, SATA SSDs are affordable alternatives compared to other SSD types.

mSATA SSD

Smaller and thinner than the 2.5-inch format, mSATA SSDs utilize the SATA interface for connection. These SSDs are smaller than 2.5-inch SSDs and are commonly used in laptops, ultrabooks, and embedded systems where space is limited. mSATA SSD has a maximum bandwidth of 6 Gbps and the read/write speeds can reach up to 551 Mbps and 304 Mbps.

M.2 SATA SSD

M.2 SSDs are small, thin circuit boards that connect directly to the motherboard via an M.2 slot. While the M.2 form factor can house both SATA and NVMe SSDs, M.2 SATA specifically uses the SATA interface for connection. The compact design of these SSDs, makes them ideal for laptops and desktops as well. M.2 SSDs are available in different lengths and storage capacities, providing users with a compact and efficient storage solution for their computing needs.

M.2 NVMe SSD

Similar to the M.2 SATA SSD, the M.2 NVMe SSD utilizes the same compact M.2 form factor but an NVMe PCIe interface for data transfer. NVMe SSDs offer significantly faster read and write speeds compared to SATA SSDs, thanks to their use of the PCIe interface. They provide significant speed improvements compared to both 2.5-inch SATA SSDs and M.2 SATA SSDs. With read speeds of up to 7,400 MB/s and write speeds of up to 7,000 MB/s, this type of M.2 SSD is perfect for tasks like video editing, 3D rendering, and high-end gaming.

SSDs offer several advantages over other types of internal storage. Whether you are looking to buy a 2.5 inch SSD or an M.2 SSD, the following key benefits make it a value-for-money purchase:

Faster Read/Write Speed

One of the major advantages of using SSD is the fast read-write speed. SSD can provide up to 10 times faster read speeds and 20 times faster storage or write speeds compared to traditional hard disk drives. SSD can improve the boot times, application loading, and file transfers and offer smooth performance. The Blazing fast speed of SSDs makes them best storage option for high-end laptops, gaming PCs, workstations, and servers.

Durability

SSDs are more durable than HDDs because they have no moving parts. SSDs are built from silicon memory chips and turn up to be more resistant to physical shock, vibration, and wear and tear. With a thin profile and low weight, M.2 SSDs tend to be more suitable for laptops that are frequently moved around.

Silent Operation

As SSDs don’t have any moving parts, they operate silently unlike HDDs. Both the SATA SSD and NVMe SSD provide a silent environment whereas hard disks produce sounds of whirring and vibration. The quieter computing environments make SSDs more suitable for offices and homes.

Energy Efficiency

SSD requires less power to operate compared to an HDD. Using an SSD can benefit by providing longer battery life in laptops and reduced electricity bills in desktop PC and data centers.
