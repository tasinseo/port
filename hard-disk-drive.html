Popular Types of Hard Disk for Gaming, Surveillance, Server, and RAID 

Hard disks are best categorized based on their use case. It is crucial to have a clear idea about each type and in-between so that you can buy the right Hard Disk for gaming, NAS, or other purposes. Let’s look at how the varying types of Hard Disks serve below.

Consumer Grade

The most common type of Desktop Hard Disk or Laptop HDD is the consumer-grade one, which comes at a spectacularly low price. This is the very basic PC hard disk in the market, ideal for almost any regular home or office computer. Storing personal files, regularly opening them, and often using them as a backup drive is the most common use . You can use one of these for almost any personal computing needs, starting from productivity to light gaming and more.

Hard Drive for Gaming

You can also get a specialized hard disk drive for gaming, with a very high HDD Cache Memory for faster load times. One popular HDD brand of this type is the Firecuda series of hard drives by Seagate. These gaming desktop HDDs are fitted with internal parts to facilitate better locating and accessing data from their hard disc plates. Also, the drive shell is optimized for temperatures inside a typical high-performance computer.

Enterprise Grade

Enterprise-grade hard disk drives are made with constant use of the drive such as servers or surveillance storages. Since they require passing the test of time, the Enterprise HDDs come with robust architecture, building materials, and power-regulating properties. By making the platters ruggedly attached to the hard drive shell and fitting rubber grommets, Enterprise HDD can significantly reduce noise. It also ensures a very extended server lifespan for the drive by reducing heat, wear, and tear due to vibration. WD is a leading name for enterprise-grade desktop PC hard disk drives which they bring to the market in five distinct colors for specific needs. Read what each WD Hard Drive colors mean and which would be right for your work or home.

Surveillance HDD

One of the most common enterprise Hard Disk types offered by popular HDD brands is their Surveillance hard drives. Hard disk that are made for surveillance tasks can read & write multiple streams of data for users on multiple desktops. These hard disk drives are made in a way that significantly reduces power consumption at the cost of read/write speeds. Due to low power operations, Surveillance HDD can last for years, if not decades without hardly any maintenance. Brands like Toshiba and Seagate are highly popular for Surveillance HDD. In Bangladesh, you can buy HDD for your CCTV or other security systems at the lowest price from PORT0 Ltd.
